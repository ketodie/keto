1. What is keto diet?

Keto diet has become very popular in the recent time as it has been directly linked with weight loss and several health benefits. Keto diet is a high-fat, low carbohydrate diet with moderate level of protein which gives amazing results for weight loss and maintaining ideal body weight. Carbs are the primary source of energy in the human body and when you start a keto diet, you limit the intake of carbs and thus deprive your body of its energy source. Body then utilizes fats as its energy source and thus resulting in utilization of fat and fat loss.

The basic nutrition percentage of this diet consists of around 60% of fat, 30% protein and 10% of carbs. For example, if you are on a 2000kcal per day diet, the maximum amount of carbs you can have should be around 20g to 50g.

3. Is ketogenic diet good for your body?

Ketogenic diet is generally considered as safe for most of the body types and can work positively for almost everyone. However, there may be some instances when the keto diet may not work as expected. These instances normally occur when the person does not follow the diet strictly.

  • Standard Ketogenic diet – This is the best suited keto diet for a person who is just looking for a rapid weight loss. In this diet the user has to follow the 70% Fat:20% Protein:10% Carbs formula. A person with an overweight body can have maximum benefits with this diet.
  • Cyclical keto diet – This diet is normally followed by people who are looking to avail other health benefits or simply looking to reduce a small amount of weight. People generally follow the keto diet in short cycles and take off days where they can eat 50% of carbs in a single day.
  • Targeted keto diet – This diet is famous in athletes as this diet allows to have the maximum percentage of carbs in keto diet. People on this diet can have around 15% of carbs in a single day due to their active lifestyle which helps them burn fat at a faster rate.
